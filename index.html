About Me

I am a PhD student at the Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology, University of Technology Sydney, Australia, supervised by Dr. Lu Qin. Previously, I received my B.E. and M.E. degrees in Computer Science from Shenzhen University. My current research interests lie in the area of graph data mining.
