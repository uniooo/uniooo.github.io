About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Shenzhen University, China. I received my Ph.D. from the Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology, University of Technology Sydney, Australia, in Aug 2023. Previously, I received my B.E. and M.E. degrees in Computer Science from Shenzhen University. My current research interests lie in the area of graph data mining.
